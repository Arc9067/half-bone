Half Bone

Half Bone is a project built on the Ethereum network that aims to provide a decentralized platform for buying and selling digital assets with a unique twist. The project's key feature is a 4% buy and sell tax, which is used to buy back and burn Half Bone tokens, effectively reducing the overall supply and increasing the value of remaining tokens

How To Buy Half Bone ?

  • STEP 1

    Download trustwallet or Metamask From The App Store or Google Play Store, for free. Or add the desktop browser extension on Google Chrome by going to metamask.io. Follow their easy steps from their guide to set up a wallet.

  • STEP 2

    Send ETH (ERC) to Trustwallet | Metamask You can buy Eth directly through Trustwallet or you can transfer some from another wallet or crypto exchange. You can follow tutorials on Youtube if you get stuck on this step.

  • STEP 3

    Connect To Uniswap Go to Uniswap via a desktop browser or inside your Trustwallet wallet Swap your Eth to Bone, then swap your Bone to Half Bone
